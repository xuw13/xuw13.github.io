About Me

Great UX
starts with
empathy

Hi, I'm Xu Wang, a UI/UX designer who bridges user insight and product thinking.

My academic background began in Software Engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in coding, algorithms, and system logic. Through that experience, I became increasingly drawn to the human side of technology, leading me to pursue a deeper focus in human-computer interaction. I hold a B.S. in Software Engineering from UC Irvine and a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction & Design.

I focus on connecting user needs, product goals, and visual experience to design digital products that are both appealing and easy to use. From research to delivery, I own the full design loop: mapping mental models, untangling business logic, architecting user flows, and shipping polished interfaces. I believe great design should feel intuitive — users experience clarity, ease, and value, not the complexity behind the craft.
